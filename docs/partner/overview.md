# Partner overview
Hi, welcome to the Domits overview inside the Partner folder!
This folder contains everything you need to integrate your platform with Domits—from managing listings, availability, and reservations to accessing our key APIs and partner programs.

Inside, you’ll find:

 - A "Get Started" section for new integrators

 - Featured updates, SDKs, and tools to help you build faster

 - A central entry point for all partner guides and technical references

 - Documentation organized by integration type (PMS, Affiliates, Agents, etc.)

 - A clear "How to Integrate" flow, including compliance and certification steps

 - Contact and support information for technical or partnership assistance

Use this overview as your roadmap to a successful integration with Domits.
