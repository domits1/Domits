# Training and Awareness Policy

This policy ensures that all employees understand their information security responsibilities and receive the necessary training to protect organizational assets.

## Purpose
The purpose of this policy is to build awareness of security risks, promote responsible behavior, and ensure staff are capable of recognizing and responding to security threats.

## Scope
Applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties with access to organizational systems or information.

## Training Requirements
All personnel must:
- Complete onboarding security training before gaining system access.
- Participate in periodic refresher training on key topics such as phishing, data protection, and secure handling of information.
- Receive role-specific training when their job involves handling sensitive or critical information.

## Awareness Activities
To maintain ongoing awareness, the organization provides:
- Regular security reminders and guidance.
- Alerts about emerging threats (e.g., phishing campaigns).
- Short awareness exercises to reinforce key concepts.

## Responsibilities
- **Management:** Ensures employees receive required training.  
- **Security Team:** Develops and delivers training, tracks completion, and updates content.  
- **Employees:** Follow security guidelines and complete all mandatory training.

## Continuous Improvement
Training material is reviewed regularly and updated to reflect new risks, technologies, and regulatory requirements.
