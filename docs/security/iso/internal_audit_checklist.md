# Internal Audit  Checklist  
(Step 5: Performing the Internal Audit)

Use this checklist while conducting the internal audit to ensure the process follows ISO 27001 requirements and internal audit procedures.

---

## Audit Preparation

| Control Point | Check | How to Verify |
|----------------|--------|--------------|
| Has the internal audit plan been created? | [ ] | Verify the audit schedule, scope, objectives, and timeline. |
| Has the audit scope been clearly defined (processes, departments, controls)? | [ ] | Check audit plan or audit charter. |
| Has the audit team been assigned and are they independent? | [ ] | Validate team assignments and segregation from the audited area. |
| Are audit criteria defined (e.g., ISO 27001 clauses, Annex A controls, policies)? | [ ] | Review criteria listed in the audit plan. |

---

## Audit Execution

| Control Point | Check | How to Verify |
|----------------|--------|--------------|
| Are interviews conducted with involved process owners or staff? | [ ] | Confirm interview notes or attendance records. |
| Are documented procedures and policies reviewed? | [ ] | Compare documents against the current audit criteria. |
| Is evidence collected during the audit? | [ ] | Ensure evidence is tagged, stored, and traceable. |
| Is the effectiveness of controls evaluated (not only existence)? | [ ] | Validate that controls are tested, not just visually checked. |

---

## Nonconformity Identification

| Control Point | Check | How to Verify |
|----------------|--------|--------------|
| Are nonconformities documented clearly? | [ ] | Confirm entries in the nonconformity register. |
| Does each nonconformity include objective evidence? | [ ] | Reference evidence files or screenshots. |
| Is each nonconformity linked to an ISO clause or control? | [ ] | Check the mapping in the findings report. |
| Has the audit team verified impact and severity? | [ ] | Confirm rating matrix (major / minor). |

---

## Audit Reporting

| Control Point | Check | How to Verify |
|----------------|--------|--------------|
| Has the audit report been completed? | [ ] | Review the finalized audit report document. |
| Is the report clear, factual, and based on evidence (not opinions)? | [ ] | Validate findings and remove subjective language. |
| Has the report been reviewed and approved by the lead auditor? | [ ] | Check approval signature or version history. |
| Has the report been communicated to management and/or stakeholders? | [ ] | Confirm email, meeting notes, or acknowledgment. |

---

## Follow-Up

| Control Point | Check | How to Verify |
|----------------|--------|--------------|
| Are corrective actions assigned to owners with deadlines? | [ ] | Check the corrective action tracker. |
| Is progress monitored until closure? | [ ] | Verify status updates and follow-up meetings. |
| Has the effectiveness of corrective actions been verified? | [ ] | Confirm closure evidence or retesting. |
