# Security Review Checklist

Use this checklist during internal reviews or audits.

---

## Policy Documents

| Control Point | Check | How to verify |
|---------------|--------|---------------|
| Are all required policy documents available? | [ ] | Compare the required policy list (Information Security Policy, Risk Management, Incident Management, etc.) with the documents stored in the document management system. |
| Are all policy documents formally approved? | [ ] | Check whether each document has an approval signature, approval record, or version history confirming management approval. |
| Are policy documents reviewed periodically? | [ ] | Review the "last review date" on the documents and compare it to the required review cycle (e.g., annual review). |
| Have the policy documents been communicated to employees? | [ ] | Ask for proof of communication (training records, onboarding documentation, intranet notifications, etc.). |

---

## Security Controls

| Control Point | Check | How to verify |
|---------------|--------|---------------|
| Are required security controls implemented? | [ ] | Validate implementation via system screenshots, configuration exports, or tool dashboards. |
| Are controls tested regularly? | [ ] | Ask for evidence of penetration tests, vulnerability scans, or control test results. |
| Are access rights reviewed periodically? | [ ] | Check access review logs, user access review reports, or audit trail documentation. |
| Are deviations or exceptions documented and approved? | [ ] | Look for exception logs and ensure they have justification and approval. |

---

## Risk Management

| Control Point | Check | How to verify |
|---------------|--------|---------------|
| Are risks identified and documented? | [ ] | Review the risk register in the risk management tool or documented spreadsheet. |
| Are risks assessed using a defined methodology? | [ ] | Check if risks have impact and likelihood scoring with consistent criteria. |
| Are risk treatment plans defined and tracked? | [ ] | Look for assigned mitigation actions, owners, deadlines, and progress updates. |
| Are risks reviewed periodically by management? | [ ] | Verify meeting minutes, sign-offs, or review logs. |

---

## Continuous Improvement

| Control Point | Check | How to verify |
|---------------|--------|---------------|
| Are improvement processes in place? | [ ] | Check for corrective action procedures or improvement workflows. |
| Are corrective actions tracked until completion? | [ ] | Review action logs, improvement tracker, or project/task management tools. |
| Are lessons learned documented? | [ ] | Ask for post-incident, audit, or improvement review documents. |
| Are improvements communicated to stakeholders? | [ ] | Verify communications sent out (emails, reports, intranet posts). |

---

Use this checklist to demonstrate oversight and compliance during audits or management reviews.
