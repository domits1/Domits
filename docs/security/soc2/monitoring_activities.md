# Monitoring Activities 
Monitoring Activities ensure that an organization continuously reviews the effectiveness of its controls and identifies issues before they become security risks. This SOC 2 criterion requires ongoing oversight through activities such as internal audits, system monitoring, alerting, and regular evaluations of control performance. Effective monitoring helps detect anomalies, verify compliance, and support timely remediation, ensuring that security practices remain reliable and up to date.


## Requirements 
- [ ] Internal control reviews or audits  
- [ ] Tracking and remediation of issues  
- [ ] Logging and alerting in place  
- [ ] Evaluation of control effectiveness  
- [ ] Continuous improvement processes
